Session Overview

Professor Thaldor told me I should keep a journal of my times here at Cinderwind College. I do not necessarily see the need for such frivoly but I will play the good student and much to my surprise, I like the old man, he has much knowledge and is willing to share it.

If this is to be my record of my time here the I will start with getting accepted. I was deep in the forest of Jakunta hunting for some of the rare spell components that can be found in this uniquely magical forest. While wandering I encountered many Blood Hawks in the area and was very wary of attacks from the sky, I think that is why I noticed the bird straight away. He dove out of the sky with letter clamped in talon, I took the note from bird and sent it on its way. Havin received my admission letter, my plan could begin. I immediately turned toward the direct of school and started my long journey.

Many days later I arrived on campus, fortunately on very first day of school. Approaching such a creation of foot is a wonder to behold, the entire campus floating in the air suspended by magic that has been lost for so long. Once on grounds, I was led to the Dorms, which also house the Dinning Hall, and was sat at the first year table, where I was slowly introduced to my new companions. I do not remember who came in when, but slowly all of my fellows took their seats at the table with me. Gregor and I kept mostly to ourselves but we did make note of the large dragonborn, I now know that to be Balgor, who sat next to us. Sylva did try to start up some conversation but none of us were much for it.

As dinner came to an end, we new students were asked to stay behind, as everyone left, for some ground rules and introductions. After the hall emptied out except for Thaldor, he started to give us some basic introductions and guidelines of life here. However, he suddenly gasped and looked up into the rafters of the room. We all turned to see what he was looking at. I have seen some large spiders in my day, this made those look reasonable. The spider dropped down from the rafters and landed on a table in the center of the room. We immediately started trying to dispatch the creature but did have some struggles with doing so. I'm not proud to say that I spend a large portion of the fight caught in some web it threw my way. I did notice one of my new friends, Orianna, snuck off and managed to pick the entry door. I will have to keep these skills in mind for some... future needs.

Thaldor appeared back in the room and calmed the spider before we fully killed it, apparently this is some type of hazing that they do for all new students. I don't know how I feel about that but it is good to know we will be expected to overcome challenges on our own, might make the staff look the other way to students taking matters into their own hands. Thaldor then finished up his introduction by telling us we will face many challenges like this during our time here at the school and he looks forward to us finding our way here at Cinderwind College.

Finally I was able to leave the dinning hall and we were shown to our new quarters for our residency. They were nothing special but they are a great deal better than sleeping under a tree or rock ledge. Gregor, damn the softy, immediatly settled in and got comfortable. I will have to make sure he does not slack too much. On the bed in my room I did find an envelope with some interesting tokens inside. However, as i poured them out into my hand they immediately seemed to evaporate. As they did so, I felt my connection to the weave strengthen. I immediately tried to cast one of the spells I had been experimenting with and found that where it had previously been failing, it now could sustain itself and form succesfully. I am very intrigued to see what is going on at this school and to find what secrets it is hiding.
