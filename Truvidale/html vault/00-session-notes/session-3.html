Session Overview

This session starts 2 weeks after Session 2 in game. During those 2 weeks we went to classes like normal and got to know each other a bit better.

Vladik and Orianna seemed to strike up a broship, grabbing drinks and exchanging some small talk.

Fenna and Alice also seem to be spending time together and building a friendship.

The first thing we dive into is our first Religion and Healing class with Joleene Cybin, the new elf teacher this year. Vladik and Fenna both take a moment to make introductions and get to know her a little bit.

We then jump towards the end of the second week of school and pick up with Vladik and Orianna in the Student Center. They are sitting around drinking and having a good time when Vladik asks if Orianna thinks it odd that we are not allowed in the Faculty Offices. Orianna agrees and Vladik proposes that maybe they could get in.

The pair then set off towards the offices. Vladik uses Disguise Self to appear as the rogue teacher Ax Tabor. As the pair approach the front door they run into Arith. Through some amazing use of Minor Illusion, Vladik begins explaining to Arith that he is getting some extra materials for Orianna because she is showing particular aptitude for his class. All was going okay if not great until Orianna decided to make it look like she and Ax Tabor were up to possibly more salacious activities. This made Arith suspicious and Vladik, not wanting this plan to fail, decided to cast Suggestion on Arith, telling him to leave them alone and take a walk around the campus.
Having narrowly avoided disaster, the pair continue on their way. After failing to remember that Thaumaturgy can unlock doors and unsuccessfully picking the lock, they finally make it into the offices. Vladik first walks over to Thaldor's room and knocks to see if the professor is in. Thaldor welcomes Vladik, looking like Ax Tabor, into his office and begins to discuss. Vladik was hoping he was out so he panics and leaves rather quickly.
Deciding they have taken enough risk the pair begin to leave when Thaldor exits his office. He then heads down a different hallway which has a single door at the far end. He unlocks the door with what sounds like a very sturdy lock and key and slips inside not letting anyone see past him.

Next we are in one of Thaldor's classes where he is trying to teach us about cantrips and magic. He splits us off into 2 groups, casters (Vladik, Fenna, and Orianna) and non-casters (Balgor, Sylva, and Alice). The goal is just to show off what you can do with magic. The caster group does almost nothing with their magic either not working how they intended or not working at all. On the other hand, Alice discovers that she can cast magic innately and moves to join the caster group.

The group heads out of the class toward the center of campus, as they walk they are discussing Alice's new revelation. Upon reaching the plaza, the group finds Sylva cornering Brad and asking about magic and where it comes from. She seems to grow increasingly heated and begins yelling. Suddenly, magic seems to fall off of her and she no longer looks like herself, but like a large Goblin. The group immediately begins to try to subdue the new foe, who has managed to knock out Brad and is seemingly getting ready to engage with anyone around.
After a short fight with many Magic Missiles from both Vladik and Alice, as well as more goblins appearing from around the area, the first-years manage to take out the backup and restrain the main Goblin for questioning. His name is get-name-from-adam and he was sent here by a Frost Giant named Vulkara. Vulkara is looking for the source of magic she seems to think is located at Cinderwind College. get-name-from-adam does not provide any further information to the group and Alice kills him.

After the groups close encounter, Arith arrives on the scene and sees the group of first-years surrounded by dead goblins and an unconscious Brad. He seems fairly upset by the events he sees and send the students back to their dorm. At the dorm the group decide to look in Sylva's room to see if they find any clues as to where she is. Instead they find Sylva, tied up and gagged on her bed. After making sure that she is okay, they begin to ask her some questions to figure out what happened.
She left campus 2 weeks ago to head home after not having the best first week on campus. On the way, it seems like she was attacked and kidnapped by this group of goblins and thrown in a bag. They brought her back to campus and used her knowledge to disguise themselves as her and go to her classes. She doesn't know much else than that because they kept her hidden away so the group leaves her alone. As they get ready to leave, Vladik notices a bag on her bed and asks if its hers and if he can take a look. She says that's fine, and after picking it up and looking, he realizes he is holding a Bag of Holding. Vladik, who's goal is to find and study the magic that the campus holds, does not share this knowledge with the group out of concern for relenting the bag.

All the first years head to their rooms where they find notes from Thaldor commending them on their show of skills today but also warning them to consider their actions before jumping into situations.

Summary

Summary:: Vladik and Orianna successfully sneak into the faculty offices and snoop around. The first-years are put through their first practical magic class with Thaldor where Alice discovers she is more intune with the weave than she first thought. Finally the group discovers Sylva was turned into a Goblin!! Just kidding, a goblin had been using magic for the last week to look like her and try to infiltrate Cinderwind. After dispatching the goblin and finding out some interesting information about who sent him the group heads back to the dorms and tries to help Sylva.
