Session Overview

After exiting the Cave of Knowledge, thoroughly surprising the guards at the entrance, and then handing them a badger that was summoned from Orianna's Bag of Tricks, Gray, we decided to head back towards Tinkford to get a good night's rest and let Gnome Mommy know what we found.
Upon reaching Tinkford, we decide that these people can't know the truth apparently so we instead tell them that there was a magical badger that was being protected in the cave and for some reason Fenna, despite being the nicest of us, decides to try and convince the town that this badger is some magical being that they need to take care of and worship. After this debacle, we do get Gnome Mommy alone in our tent and let her know about the magic item that we found in the center room and what it did for us. After she left the tent I decided to cast clairvoyance to look into her tent for any potential signs of magic items.

The next morning we head out of the town and begin our couple days journey north to Whispering Grove. The first day goes over with some realative ease, we encountered some dead evil bears that tried to mug us but with some use of Hypnotic Pattern were able to kill them. In a surprise turn of events, William was able to kill off all the bears.
At some point in here we also almost all fell into a hole. Luckily there were some leaves nearby that we were able to use to cleanse ourselves of the poision and move on.
Following the bears we came to a large pond that a tributary flowed through. Upon examining the water Fenna got the feeling that something was waiting to attack us. Everyone piled into the Bag of Holding and I used levitate to fly myself and the bag up and over the lake. As I reached about halfway across the lake, 2 bullywugs jumped out of the water and tried to grab me but I was too high up.

We spent the night out in the woods and then finished off our journey the next day. As we go to bed outside the Whispering Grove I cast clairvoyance again to get a view of what awaits us in the center of this grove. I see a large lumbering mold/bone creature that seems to be patrolling the area around the main tree. We all prepare to go into the grove to fight this creature.
After some rough planning that involved bad communication and a lot of butt slapping we fight the mold guy and his minions.

Upon defeating them, one of the trees surrounding the grove steps forward and slowly transforms into the form of Thaynera. She then explains to us that she is using the last of her strength to form this image and some minor protection of the area. Something is wrong with the current Thorn Mother and that Fenna needs to step in and save Grethwyl. Thaynera then gives each of us a blessing in order to help us with our fight against the Thorn Mother.
