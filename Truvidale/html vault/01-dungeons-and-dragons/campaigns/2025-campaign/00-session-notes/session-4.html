Session Overview

Saturday, Week 3

The session started the morning after the fight with Sylva Goblin. As we all awoke and came out into the common area we saw a note hung on the notice board that said "Students are to meet in the dinning hall at 9:30am for message from faculty.". We took a few minutes before heading out to discuss the events of the day before and what they might mean for us and campus. Balgor seemed to be pretty angry and after asking, he revealed that he was upset at either himself or all of us for not realizing Sylva was a goblin for 2 whole weeks. We tried to tell him that if we had noticed we obviously would have done something but it didn't seem to help his mood.

9:30 rolled around and we were all waiting in the dinning hall for the staff to arrive. As they walked in all of the faculty seemed pretty off-put and concerned with the events of yesterday and I notice that Arith seems even more upset about something. Thaldor Coldforce takes a minute to discuss the events and what happened to Sylva before informing us that campus security has been increased and the floating platform up and down from campus has been closed for the time being.

After Thaldor's message we all head back to the student dorms where we find a new note on the board "First Year Battle Royale - 12AM - Coliseum". Upon seeing this, Vladik decided to take the afternoon to study up on some spells to prepare for what he guessed was coming. Fenna, Orianna, and Alice all went over to the Coliseum to get in some sword training with Arith.

Upon their return to the dorms later that afternoon, Vladik proposes to the whole group that they could make the Battle Royale a bit more interesting by splitting into teams. Sylva tells them she has no plan to go tonight after what she had been through, and Balgor was not around at the time. The 4 decided to draw straws for teams and ended up with Fenna and Vladik against Alice and Orianna. Midnight rolls around and the group heads over to the Coliseum.

When they arrive they find a couple 4th year students waiting for them Katy and Katie. They welcome in the group and explain the rules of what is going to happen. There will be a selection of magical items available for everyone to choose from and then it is free-for-all combat. The last person standing gets to take an item of their choosing but the rest stay. After quickly picking out some items, combat begins. Many Counterspells, and missed attacks later, Alice came out the victor and keeps for herself a Ring of Protection.

The bloodied and beaten first years all returned to the dorms and went to sleep.

Sunday, Week 4

All the first years are awoken first thing in the morning by yelling and screaming from a higher floor. As they take off up the stairs they pass Sabrina, Katie, and Katy all coming down. Arriving on the 4th year floor everyone see a large Black Pudding in the middle of the room. As it falls from the ceiling and hits the floor the students decide to engage with it. They quickly learn to not pierce it or hit it with lightning as these both seem to make it split instead of hurting it. The group now has to deal with 4 smaller puddings that are causing damage to not only them, but their gear as well. Then as the combat seems to be slowing, a second pudding appears from down the hall and begins attacking Vladik.

After dealing with all the monsters; Trevalia, Sabrina, Katie, and Katy all come up the stairs. Trevalia looks disturbed at the situation, makes sure the students are okay, and then takes the damaged gear to be repaired. Vladik tries to walk down the hallway to see where the puddings came from but is stopped by Sabrina telling him not to go into her room. After a brief interaction where Orianna was causing problems and Vladik put her to sleep, Sabrina goes into her room and returns with a box and a letter. She explains that the box was given to her by a halfling outside of campus with instructions to deliver to Thaldor. Sabrina decided to take a peak in the box and that is when the puddings jumped out. She hands both the box and letter to Vladik who picks up the sleeping Orianna and decides to go investigate the letter.

Taking the letter to his room, Vladik first explains to Orianna why he put her to sleep and shows her what having a conversation got them; evidence. Taking 10 minutes to cast Detect Magic to see if the letter is a trap as well, Vladik opens it once he knows it to be safe. The letter is addressed to Thaldor and is signed "V" whom Vladik assumes to be Vulkara. Alice and Fenna then approach Vladik's room and it sounds like they may have been trying to spy but Vladik decides to let them in and explain what he has.

Session Ends

Summary

The session began with the first-year students reflecting on the previous day's events involving Sylva, a goblin in disguise, and attending a faculty meeting where campus security was heightened. They learned about a "First Year Battle Royale" scheduled for midnight at the Coliseum. After splitting into teams, Vladik and Fenna faced off against Alice and Orianna in a free-for-all combat where Alice emerged victorious, claiming a Ring of Protection. The next morning, chaos ensued as the group encountered a Black Pudding on the fourth-year floor, leading to a challenging battle against multiple smaller puddings. After defeating the creatures, they learned from Sabrina that her mysterious box led to the chaos; it contained a letter addressed to Thaldor Coldforce from someone named "V." Vladik decided to investigate further with Orianna while Alice and Fenna approached to inquire about the situation.
